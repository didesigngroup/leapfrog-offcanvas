Ad Specs - Promo 1

Promo 1a
Background image that gets cropped + live text
  • 767 x 200 image
  • Character restricted text
Promo 1b
Background pattern or gradient that gets cropped + live text + image. Image is hidden at narrow widths.
  • Background pattern or gradient
  • 150 x 100 image
  • Character restricted text
Promo 1c
Two predesigned ads supplied as images. Two variations so that fixed text is readable at all screen sizes. Images swapped at different viewports with the picture element. Design exploration may be needed to determine range of text-size to recommend for legibility at all widths.
  • 800 x 200 ad for column widths 480-767
  • 480 x 150 ad for column widths up to 480

Ad Specs - Promo 2

Promo 2a
Full bleed image + text + button. Stacked or horizontal display depending on width. Some art direction required since image is cropped horizontally at smaller range of horizontal layout.
  • 400 x 300 image
  • Character restricted text
Promo 2b
Similar to Promo 2a, but without the horizontal cropping, so no art direction required. Image won't be full bleed for all widths of horizontal layout.
  • 400 x 300 image
  • Character restricted text
Promo 2c
Two predesigned ads supplied as images: one vertical and one horizontal. Images swapped at different viewports with the picture element. Design exploration may be needed to determine range of text-size to recommend for legibility at all widths.
  • 800 x 400 for column widths 480-767
  • 400 x 600 for column widths up to 480
