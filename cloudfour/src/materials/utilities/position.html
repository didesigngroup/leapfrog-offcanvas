--- notes: | + `.u-pos-absolute` sets `position` to `absolute`. + `.u-pos-absolute-center` positions an element to the center of its parent. + `.u-pos-absolute-left` positions an element to the left of its parent, vertically centered. + `.u-pos-absolute-right` positions an element to the right of its parent, vertically centered. + `.u-pos-fixed` sets `position` to `fixed`. + `.u-pos-relative` sets `position` to `relative`. + `.u-pos-static` sets `position` to `static`. ---
