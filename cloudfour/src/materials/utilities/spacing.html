--- notes: | Spacing utilities provide a way to apply normalized whitespace in the form of `margin` or `padding`. Many of the [Bootstrap 4 spacing utilities](http://v4-alpha.getbootstrap.com/components/utilities/#spacing) have been included in this pattern library. In addition, a few extra classes have been provided for responsive padding: + `.p-a-responsive` adds responsive padding to all sides. + `.p-x-responsive` adds responsive padding to the left and right sides. + `.p-y-responsive` adds responsive padding to the top and bottom sides. ---
