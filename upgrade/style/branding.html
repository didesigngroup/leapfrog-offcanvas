Branding

Financial institutions are allowed to select a primary accent color for online banking. It must meet the WCAG contrast ratio for AA compliance with accessibility guidelines. The default color for links & icons is #0055CC. Test color compliance at this site: http://snook.ca/technical/colour_contrast/colour.html
